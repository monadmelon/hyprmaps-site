Reimagining Tourism

We're building the digital infrastructure for the next generation of travel.

The Mission

HyprMaps is an open-source initiative to create a decentralized, community-owned tourism platform. We believe that local businesses and tourism boards should own their data and their destiny, not be beholden to extractive global platforms.

By combining modern web technology with a "local-first" philosophy, we are building a system that keeps value within the community while providing travelers with a superior, authentic experience.

How It Works

For Travelers

A seamless, ad-free experience that works offline. Discover hidden gems verified by locals, not algorithms.

For Businesses

Zero commission fees. Direct connection to customers. Simple tools to manage your digital presence.

For Destinations

Real-time data insights. Ability to guide tourist flow. A sovereign platform that you control.

What We Believe

Community Ownership

Local stakeholders should control their digital infrastructure, not foreign corporations.

Local Knowledge

Curated, verified local insights are more valuable than algorithmic recommendations.

Open Infrastructure

We build on open standards to prevent vendor lock-in and ensure long-term sustainability.

Open to Collaboration

Whether you're a tourism board, a developer, or an impact investor, we'd love to talk about the future of sustainable tourism.
